Ethan Mikael

Ethan Mikael is a Los Angeles based actor well known for playing quirky characters in his home town of San Diego.

“[Ethan Mikael] Tapley’s fidgety Christopher Wren... pushes the quirk level to a new high... he’s fun to watch.” -Jean Lowerison, SDGLN Theater Critic

“Mr. [Ethan Mikael] Tapley and Mr. Cole’s chemistry keep engagement levels high.” -Bill Eadie, San Diego Story

With a penchant for voices and accents Ethan makes transforming into a role look effortless. As well as being an actor, Ethan writes and is an avid photographer.
